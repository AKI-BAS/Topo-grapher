About Topo-grapher

Topo-grapher makes accurate site topography accessible to architects, BIM managers, and landscape architects — no GIS software, no surveyor, no coordinate-system wrangling. Draw your site on a map and download terrain ready for Revit, SketchUp, Rhino, or ArchiCAD.

Why it exists

Getting real site terrain into design software has always been disproportionately painful: the data lives in GIS formats, behind portals, in coordinate systems that don't match your model. Most architects either pay for a survey, fight with GIS tools, or model the ground by eye. Topo-grapher exists to collapse that into a few clicks — pulling the best open government elevation data for your location and converting it straight into the format your software wants.

What it does

Topo-grapher sources high-resolution open elevation data — down to 0.4m in Denmark and 1m across much of Europe and the US — and exports it as XYZ CSV, IFC, and DXF. It also adds building footprints and a site reference for orientation. It runs entirely in the browser, with a free tier that needs no account.
